Why Tantra Belongs

Presence. Honesty. Relationship.

Tantra is one of the most misunderstood practices. It's about something deeper: presence, embodiment, consciousness, truth, energy, boundaries and relationship.

That's why it connects so strongly with leadership: It's about how you hold tension, react under pressure, stay connected to yourself, how clearly you perceive others.

It's about being more present, honest and whole in relationship. First with yourself — and from there with others.
