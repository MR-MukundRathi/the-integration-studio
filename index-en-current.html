What it's about

Development that doesn't
only happen in your head

The Integration Studio is a space for people who don't just want to understand — but truly embody change in their leadership, their presence, and their relationship with themselves and others.

At its core, it's about integration: of inner world and outer impact, of clarity and feeling, of body and consciousness.

The Foundation

Four Principles of My Work

01

Integration over Insight

Understanding alone isn't enough — development becomes effective when it arrives in experience and behavior.

02

Embodiment over Concept

It's not just about knowledge, but about how something shows up in your body and actions.

03

Clear Container over Control

Development needs clear structure — and simultaneously the freedom for real processes to emerge.

04

Process over Quick Fix

Sustainable change emerges through genuine processes, not through short-term solutions.

100 Days Embodied Leadership Mentoring

An intensive mentoring program for leaders who want to truly embody leadership — not just reflect on it.

  • Weekly 1:1 sessions over 100 days for deep personal work
  • Embodiment practices: breath work, somatic exercises, presence training
  • Leadership in context: decision-making under pressure, authentic communication
  • Team dynamics: understanding patterns, holding tension, creating psychological safety
  • Integration rituals: translating insights into sustainable behavioral change
  • Available as individual mentoring or in small groups (max. 6 participants)
