The Approach

Development that goes beyond tools

My background is in leadership and business context — with real experience in responsibility, team dynamics and developing people.

The work deliberately includes what often has no space: body work, awareness, connection and inner processes are not an add-on, but integral components.

This doesn't create a classic coaching offering, but rather: Development spaces for people who want to not just understand change, but embody it.
