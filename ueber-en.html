The Person

My path. My work.

My background is in leadership and business — with real experience in responsibility, team dynamics and developing people.

But the work deliberately goes deeper. It includes what often has no space in professional contexts: body work, awareness, connection and inner processes.

These elements aren't an add-on for me — they're integral parts of genuine development. That's where The Integration Studio came from.
